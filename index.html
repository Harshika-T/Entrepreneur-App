    Common Diets







    Some common diet plans include Keto, Vegan, DASH, Low-fat Diet, and much more. The Keto Diet is all about burning fats in a person's body, than carbohydrate, it is good for children becuase it can control epilepsy in children. The Vegan diet is about is not using any type of animal prodcts as food, and using only food made from plants, it also restricting eggs and milk. The DASH diet is used to control blood pressure, and is high in potassium, calcium, and magnesium.
